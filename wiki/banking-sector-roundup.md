# BCG Banking Sector Roundup

Boston Consulting Group's quarterly / half-yearly / annual digest on the
Indian banking sector. Six editions ingested, covering H1 FY25 through
9M FY26.

## Panel

37 banks (36 in early FY25 editions): 12 PSU, 10 Private-New, 10 Private-
Old, 4–5 Small Finance. Roster published on page 1 of every deck. See
[[indian-banking-fy25-fy26]] for cross-period themes.

## Read in one paragraph

Asset growth has been steady through FY25 and into FY26: deposits at
+10–12% YoY, advances at +11–13%, with MSME advances consistently
fastest (+14–18%). The income story has weakened across FY26 — total
income YoY decelerated from +13% (FY25) to +7% (9M FY26), driven by NII
compression (FY26 prints 2–3% YoY growth versus FY25's 8–10%) as NIMs
fell ~20–27 bps YoY. Non-interest income carried the top line in FY26,
growing 22–44% YoY. PAT growth fell from +16–20% in FY25 to +3–6% in
FY26. Credit quality kept improving (GNPA from 2.6% → 1.9%); CASA kept
falling (37.1% → 35.9%) and ROA softened from 1.4% to 1.3%.

## Caveats

- Panel size shifted (Small Finance went from 4 to 5 mid-FY25), so
  pre/post-FY25 totals aren't strictly comparable.
- Numbers are BCG's aggregate from Capitaline + bank disclosures.
  Original filings are ground truth for any specific number.
- Only the page-2 snapshot has been ingested. Per-bank tables, segment
  deep-dives, and themes from later pages of each deck are NOT in the
  wiki yet.
