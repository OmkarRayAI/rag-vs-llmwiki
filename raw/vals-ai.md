# Vals AI

Source: https://www.vals.ai/home

Vals AI is an independent AI model benchmarking platform. It positions itself
as "Independent Evaluation, Unbiased Benchmarks" and emphasizes testing AI on
real-world tasks. Many of its benchmarks are developed in-house.

Benchmarks are organized by domain:
- Coding: SWE-bench Verified, Terminal-Bench 2.1, Vibe Code Bench,
  LiveCodeBench, IOI.
- Finance: Finance Agent v2, CorpFin v2, MortgageTax, TaxEval v2.
- Healthcare: MedCode, MedScribe.
- Legal: LegalBench.
- Math and academic: ProofBench, GPQA Diamond, MMLU Pro, MMMU.
- Aggregate indices: Vals Index, Vals Multimodal Index.

Models tracked include releases from Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, xAI, DeepSeek,
Alibaba, Moonshot AI, MiniMax, Cohere, and zAI.

Offerings include leaderboards, per-model pages, side-by-side comparisons, a
Model Guide, App Reports, industry-filtered rankings, performance-over-time
charts, and Pareto cost-versus-accuracy views. Methodology documentation is
published alongside the leaderboards. The platform has been cited in The New
York Times and The Wall Street Journal.

As of fetch on 2026-05-30, Claude Opus 4.8 led the Vals Index at 70.17%,
followed by GPT 5.5 and Claude Opus 4.7.
