# BCG Banking Sector Roundup (Series Notes)

Publisher: Boston Consulting Group (BCG).
Series: "Banking Sector Roundup" — quarterly / half-yearly / annual reports
on Indian scheduled commercial banks.

PDFs in `raw/` (all © 2024 Boston Consulting Group):
- `banking-sector-roundup-h1fy25-vf.pdf` — H1 FY25 (54 pages)
- `banking-sector-roundup-9mfy25.pdf` — 9M FY25 (48 pages)
- `banking-sector-roundup-fy25.pdf` — FY25 full year (55 pages)
- `banking-sector-roundup-q1fy26.pdf` — Q1 FY26 (48 pages)
- `banking-sector-roundup-h1fy26.pdf` — H1 FY26 (49 pages)
- `banking-sector-roundup-9mfy26.pdf` — 9M FY26 (48 pages)

## Coverage

Each report covers ~36–37 Indian banks segmented as:
- PSU (12 banks): SBI, PNB, Bank of Baroda, Union Bank, Canara, Bank of
  India, Indian Bank, Central Bank, Indian Overseas, UCO, Bank of
  Maharashtra, Punjab & Sind.
- Private — New (10): HDFC, ICICI, Axis, Kotak, IndusInd, IDFC First,
  Yes, IDBI, Bandhan, RBL.
- Private — Old (10): Federal, J&K, South Indian, Karnataka, Dhanlaxmi,
  Karur Vysya, City Union, DCB, CSB, TMB.
- Small Finance: 4 banks in H1 FY25 / 9M FY25 (AU, Ujjivan, Equitas,
  Suryoday); 5 from FY25 onward (adds Jana SFB).

## Source data

BCG analysis on top of Capitaline, Financial Results, and Investor
Presentations. Page 2 of every deck is the "industry snapshot" — totals
and key ratios for the period — and is the primary cross-period
data point captured below.

## Methodology caveats

- Reports are point-in-time aggregations of the 36–37-bank panel.
  Composition shifts: Small Finance bank count moved from 4 to 5 mid-
  FY25, so totals across the panel are not strictly comparable between
  H1/9M FY25 and FY25 onward.
- All figures sourced from BCG's analysis of Capitaline data and
  bank-published results / investor presentations. Original bank
  filings would be the ground truth for any specific number.
- The page-2 snapshot is the headline view; per-bank breakdowns,
  segment deep-dives, and themes are in the rest of each deck and
  have NOT been ingested into this summary.
